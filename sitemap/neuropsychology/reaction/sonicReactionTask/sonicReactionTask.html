ABOUT

The Auditory Reaction Task provides a base reaction rate to sound. This reaction speed can be used in comparison with reaction speeds of other (more complex) tasks.


TASK SPECIFICATION:

What the task measures.

  • The average response time, in milliseconds (ms), to the sound.
  • The quickest response time, in milliseconds (ms), to the sound.

    • PREQUISITES:

    To get the best out of this study:

  • Ensure that you are seated in a quite environment.
  • Ensure that the volume on your device is at the highest setting.
  • If you wear a hearing aid, feel free to compare your response times with and without them on.

    • The Audio Reaction Task should not be used for diagnostic purposes, it is intended to provide you with a basic description of reaction to sound.

    If you are experiencing auditory symptoms (such as hearing loss) please consult an audiologist.
