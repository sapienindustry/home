ABOUT

The Stop Signal Task (SST) is a contemporary instrument used in neuropsychology to facilitate inhibitory control over reactive behaviour.

TASK SPECIFICATION:

What the task measures.

  • An estimate of the time, in milliseconds (ms), it takes to prevent a response after presentation of a stop signal. This is called the Stop Signal Reaction Time (SSRT).
  • Mean reaction time (in ms) to prompts without stop signals.
  • The range of the Stop Signal Delay, in milliseconds (ms), at which your responses were accurate.
