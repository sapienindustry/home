Neuropsychology is a branch of psychology that focuses on how the structure and function of the brain relate to specific psychological processes and behaviors.

Neuropsychologists study how the brain impacts mental processes by examining changes in behavior and cognition after brain injury, disease and medication.

Neuropsychological research then develops this understanding of cognitive functioning so that we can understand how we process information when we chase our ambitions.
