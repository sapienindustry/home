ABOUT

An audiometer allows a person to measure their hearing sensitivity.

TASK SPECIFICATION:

What the task measures.

  • The minimum and maximum frequencies in Hertz (Hz) that you can hear (unilaterally and bilaterally).
  • The lowest volume at which you can detect these frequencies (unilaterally and bilaterally).

    • PREQUISITES:

    To get the best out of this study:

  • Connect a headset device.
  • Ensure you are seated in a quite environment.
  • Ensure the volume on your device is turned all the way up.
  • If you are wearing hearing aids, it is recommended that you remove them.

    • This audiometer should not be used for diagnostic purposes, it is intended to provide you with a basic description of your hearing sensitivity.
    If you are experiencing auditory symptoms (such as hearing loss) please consult an audiologist.
