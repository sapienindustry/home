ABOUT

The Colour Perception Task measures the amount of light intensity it takes for you to notice a change in colour.

TASK SPECIFICATION:

What the task measures.

  • The minimum light intensity per RGB colour at which you perceive a change in colour.
  • The maximum light intensity per RGB colour at which you perceive a change in colour.
  • The deviation of light intensity at which you perceive a change in saturated colour.

    • This task has 12 trials and should take no longer than 3 minutes to complete.

    PREQUISITES:

    To get the best out of this study:

  • Ensure that you can see your screen clearly.
  • Set your screen to the maximum brightness
  • If you wear prescription glasses, feel free to compare your results with and without them on.

    • The Colour Perception Task should not be used for diagnostic purposes, it is intended to provide you with a basic description of visual sensitivity. If you are experiencing visual symptoms (such as sight loss) please consult an optometrist.
